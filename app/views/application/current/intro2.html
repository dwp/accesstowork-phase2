2. What you'll get

Access to Work can pay for things like:

  • things to help you do your job, eg computers or software
  • special furniture, eg chairs or ramps
  • travel to and from work
  • a support worker, eg a sign language (BSL) interpreter
  • training for your colleagues to help them understand your disability

Maximum grant

You'll get as much help as you need to do your job up to a cost of £41,400 per year.

If you already have Access to Work and it's worth more it'll be reduced in April 2018.
