Using email

If you choose to use email to manage your application it may involve discussing your condition and personal situation with an agent electronically.

Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) can't guarantee external emails against:

  • loss or non-delivery
  • theft by third parties
  • manipulation of data

Asking DWP to use email to get in touch with you in this application means you understand this risk. If you have any concerns then don't include your email address.
