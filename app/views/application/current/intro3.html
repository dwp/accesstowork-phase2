3. Apply

To get an Access to Work grant you must:

  • be 16 or over
  • live and work in England, Scotland or Wales - there's a different system in Northern Ireland
  • have a job
  • be disabled or have a long-term health condition

When you apply:

  • Department for Work and Pensions will contact your employer
  • someone might visit you at work

If you have a mental health condition nobody will contact your employer without talking to you first.

Long-term health conditions

If you're not permanently disabled, your health condition must either:

  • have already lasted for at least a year
  • be expected to last at least a year

If you're permanently disabled or have a mental health condition it doesn't matter how long you've had your condition.
