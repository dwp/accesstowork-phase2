1. Overview

You can apply for help from Access to Work if you have a disability or health condition that makes it difficult for you to do parts of your job.

The grant can pay for special equipment or support to help you:

  • do things you have to do at work, eg answer the phone, go to meetings
  • get to and from work

Other benefits

You might not get Access to Work if you already get:

  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Job Seekers Allowance
  • Employment and Support Allowance
  • Severe Disability Allowance
  • Income Support
  • National Insurance Credits
