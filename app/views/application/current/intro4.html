4. Report changes

If you already have an Access to Work grant you must report anything that changes. This includes if:

  • your job role changes
  • you move house
  • you move offices or place of work
  • your condition changes
  • anything else changes that you think affects your grant

Use this service to tell Access to Work something's changed.

You'll need your grant reference number, find this on your original offer letter.

Once you report a change someone will look at it and decide if the change affects your grant and get in contact if needed. If you don't hear anything your grant won't be affected.

If it makes a major change to your grant you may have to make a full application and have another workplace assessment.
