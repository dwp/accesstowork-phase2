Hypothesis

If we make the Management Information details available and easy to read,
then advisers, team leaders and performance staff can easily view the current status of the service,
because having this information visible and easy to read will enable all staff to monitor performance

If we change the naming of parts of the service,
then we will ensure commonality and consistency across the service,
because having different names/titles for the same entity causes confusion

Changes

Added in the MI banner to Adviser view, team leader view, regional and national views
