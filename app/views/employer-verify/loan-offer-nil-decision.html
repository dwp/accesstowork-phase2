Your application for a Budgeting Loan was unsuccessful

You’re not eligible for a loan because:

  • you owe the Social Fund more than £1,500 for Crisis Loans and Budgeting Loans
  • you or your partner haven’t been getting Income Support, income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance, income-related Employment and Support Allowance or Pension Credit for the past 6 months
  • you don’t need more than £100
  • you’re involved in industrial action

If you’re not happy with this decision

You can ask for this decision to be reviewed. Write to JobCentre Plus in the next 28 days explaining why you think the decision is wrong.

Other help you can get

You might be able to get a loan from:

  • your local council
  • the Discretionary Assistance Fund in Wales
  • the Northern Ireland Social Fund
  • the Scottish Welfare Fund
